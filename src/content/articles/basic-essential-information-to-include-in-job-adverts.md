<h3 id="">As an ABSOLUTE MINIMUM, recruiters and hiring managers please include in your job ads and posts:</h3><p id=""></p><p id="">• <strong id="">Location</strong> requirement (e.g. must be in UK or USA)</p><p id="">• Is it <strong id="">full / part time employment</strong> OR <strong id="">contract</strong> OR <strong id="">freelance</strong> (NOTE that employment is NOT the same as contract)</p><p id="">• Whether it's <strong id="">on site or remote or hybrid</strong>, and if Hybrid, a clear percentage of on-site requirement)</p><p id="">• <strong id="">Timezone</strong> requirement (e.g. Must be in CET timezone)</p><p id="">• <strong id="">Visa</strong> requirements</p><p id="">• Has any <strong id="">language</strong> requirements ( e.g. "English but German highly regarded" - this way we know it's a waste of time to apply if we don't speak German too, since in my experience at least, a German speaker was always chosen in the last round)</p><p id="">• <strong id="">Salary</strong> - At LEAST a range to within 20% (e.g. €90k - €120k depending on experience - again so we can avoid applying if your expectations are unrealistic)</p><p id=""></p><blockquote id="">Sadly, everyone leaves out this important information</blockquote><p id=""></p><h3 id="">Why it's important</h3><p id="">Good designers don't have the time or energy to chase you up about any of this</p><p id="">For applicatnts, it's absolutely infuriating to get to the END of a job application and THEN be asked if you have the 'required visa' for the job when often no mention of visa was even made in the ad, so you have NO way of knowing what visa is even meant, AND it's now obvious that you've probably wasted your time.... </p><p id=""><strong id="">It's just completely unprofessional that in 2025 people are not even starting the conversation with these essentials and THEN recruiters complain that they're inundated with applications. </strong></p><p id=""><strong id="">THIS IS WHY PEOPLE - you are not communicating effectively.</strong></p><p id=""></p><h3 id="">Other things which are ideal to mention:</h3><p id="">• The <strong id="">interview process</strong> summarised (e.g. 3 interviews)</p><p id="">• Do you expect any <strong id="">free work</strong> to be done (a.k.a. a Design challenge - many designers &amp; programmers are sick to death of these, personally I don't even bother applying for jobs that expect this. <a id="" href="https://www.artfuly.com/design-resources/how-to-deflect-design-challenges">Here's why...</a>)</p>
