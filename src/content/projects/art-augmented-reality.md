  <h2 id="">Background</h2><p id="">I had launched my own art marketplace startup, and through working with a successful artist, I learned that he would put artwork on clients walls for them to consider because it resulted in more sales. </p><p id="">This project is a digital version of that principle - to virtually put any artwork in my catalogue onto any users wall at home so that they can buy with confidence. </p><p id=""><em id="">Note: The art business was closed in 2021, and I reused the Artfuly.com domain for this website when I started freelancing again. </em></p><p id="">‍</p><h3 id="">The Concept</h3><p id="">This design idea makes use of augmented reality tech.</p><p id="">Most experiences like this on the market now rely on the user downloading an app. &nbsp;I preferred to avoid making the users download and app in instead designed the viewing experience to play out from the responsive web app -for mobile devices only.</p><p>‍</p><h3>The users experience</h3><p id="">1. Click the 'View in your room' button</p><p id="">2. Accept use of the camera</p><p id="">3. Point their mobile device at the wall to view the current artwork, and be able to swipe through the current search selection on the website, such as Colour: Red and Style: Realist.</p><p id="">4. Be able to Buy, Enquire, Follow the artist, add to their virtual collection, comment or share the artwork (in-keeping with Artfuly's strategy)</p><figure id="" class="w-richtext-figure-type-image w-richtext-align-fullwidth" style="max-width:800px" data-rt-type="image" data-rt-align="fullwidth" data-rt-max-width="800px"><div id=""><img id="" alt="__wf_reserved_inherit" src="/images/webflow-cdn/6801ec791e85d1e63dd6d381_Art-AR-1.avif" width="auto" height="auto" loading="lazy"></div></figure><figure id="" class="w-richtext-figure-type-image w-richtext-align-fullwidth" style="max-width:800px" data-rt-type="image" data-rt-align="fullwidth" data-rt-max-width="800px"><div id=""><img id="" alt="__wf_reserved_inherit" src="/images/webflow-cdn/6801ec916b86c569ea2eb21d_Art-AR-2.avif" width="auto" height="auto" loading="lazy"></div></figure>
