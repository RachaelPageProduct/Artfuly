projectInDetail: |-
  <h3>Process</h3><p>Since Luke is an old friend, and work was also slow for me, I decided to help Luke out. In around a week's worth of work I provided:</p><ol start="1"><li>An initial consultation to collect information and design the business around Luke's current responsibilities and budget constraints</li><li>Research on a payment platform suitable for hairdressers — Fresha.com, which I also set up with services, prices and durations so that the calendar could be managed properly including travel times</li><li>A Marketing Strategy to target Luke's ideal clients and create special deals — co-working professionals, stay-at-home parents, and those wanting to get 'Glammed up' for a special event</li><li>Specials designed around word-of-mouth: encouraging all 3 groups to bring a friend, to grow Luke's client base as quickly as possible</li><li>Business name research (Luke chose 'CutsByLuke.co.uk'), domain registration, domain repointing, and branded email setup</li><li>Social media advice</li><li>Preparation of printed materials for co-working venue partnerships</li><li>A subtle animation for the homepage header using Immersity AI</li><li>Short links using short.io — e.g. go.cutsbyluke.co.uk/signup</li></ol><p>‍</p><h3>Challenges</h3><p>1. The client had quite low-resolution images which I had to try and upscale in Photoshop and other AI tools.</p><p>2. Since all these tasks took quite a while, I wasn't left with much time to play around with branding, typography and colour in Figma.</p><p>3. I would have liked more time to find better AI tools for image upscaling and video generation, or to create a header using Adobe After Effects.</p>
