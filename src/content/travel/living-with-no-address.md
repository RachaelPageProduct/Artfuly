<p><em>Updated: Jun 29, 2020</em></p><p>So far, paying someone to use their address or convincing a friend is the only way I know if you're in that situation.</p><p>I found a campervan business in the Netherlands who would register, insure and pay road tax on my behalf and we have a contract between us to say that I am the legal owner (because when you register a car with the government it actually means that they are the owner, and you don't want to 'give' your vehicle away in a swindle) — so make sure you have a contract between you and the person who registers it, if they're not close friends or family.</p><p>For mail, you will need an address, anything you can negotiate that suits you. Note that PO boxes are no good for registering a car, other government stuff or receiving goods, so you still need to pretend to live at a physical address.</p><p>#noaddress</p><p>‍</p>
